About

A Global
Perspective

A visionary entrepreneur and investor based in South Florida, Nitin Solanki brings a unique global perspective shaped by his journey from Zambia to the United States. With a foundation in electrical engineering and a passion for innovation, he has built a career focused on creating value and driving growth.

Journey

From Zambia
to America

Born in Luanshya, Zambia, Nitin moved to the United States at the age of 10. His family's journey took them through Boston, Sacramento, and Redding, California, before settling in the Southeast.

His parents purchased a motel in Millen, Georgia, instilling in Nitin the entrepreneurial spirit that would define his career. He moved to Atlanta for his education, graduating from Georgia Institute of Technology in 1998 with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering.

After spending 10 years in Atlanta, he relocated to West Palm Beach in 2006, where he currently resides and continues to pursue his ventures in entrepreneurship and investment.
